What is TesTopia?

TesTopia is your smart and reliable platform for online exams. Whether you’re a student eager to take assessments or a teacher designing custom tests, TesTopia gives you all the tools you need:

  • 🧑‍🏫 Create and organize exams easily
  • 📝 Add multiple-choice and direct-answer questions
  • ⏱️ Timed exams with automatic grading
  • 🌍 Geolocation support and student tracking
  • ⚡ Seamless and responsive interface for all devices

Everything you need – fast, secure, and user-friendly. Welcome to the future of exams. Welcome to TesTopia.
